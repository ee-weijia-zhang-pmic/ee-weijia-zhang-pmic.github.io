Join Us

We are always looking for motivated researchers to join the lab. If you are excited about machine learning, optimization, or AI for science, we'd love to hear from you.

Prospective PhD Students

We admit PhD students through the Department of Computer Science graduate program. Prospective students should apply through the department's official admissions portal. If you have already applied and are interested in working with us, feel free to send an email with your CV and a brief description of your research interests.

We currently have openings for PhD students starting Fall 2027.

Postdoctoral Researchers

We occasionally have postdoc openings depending on funding and research fit. If you are interested, please send your CV, a brief research statement (1–2 pages), and two representative papers to pi@university.edu with the subject line "Postdoc Application — [Your Name]".

Undergraduate & Visiting Researchers

We welcome undergraduate researchers at XYZ University who are interested in gaining research experience. We also occasionally host visiting students and researchers. Please reach out with your CV and a short description of your background and interests.
