Join Us

We are always looking for motivated researchers to join the lab. If you are passionate about power electronics, integrated circuits, wide-bandgap devices, and AI-assisted power applications, we would be delighted to hear from you.

Prospective PhD Students

We welcome outstanding and highly motivated students with a Bachelor's or Master's degree to apply for PhD study in our group. Students with backgrounds in analog/mixed-signal IC design, power electronics, wide-bandgap semiconductor devices, circuit modeling, or related areas are especially encouraged to apply.

We value candidates with strong engineering fundamentals, curiosity for research, and the motivation to explore challenging problems in next-generation power electronics and integrated systems.

How to Apply: Interested applicants are welcome to send their CV and a brief research proposal to eewjzhang@ust.hk. Detailed admission requirements and official application procedures can be found on the HKUST postgraduate admissions website.

Research Assistants & Postdoctoral Researchers

We are also continuously recruiting Research Assistants (RA) and occasionally Postdoctoral Researchers. Candidates with strong research experience and a good fit to our research directions are encouraged to apply.

Please send your CV, research statement, and representative publications to eewjzhang@ust.hk.

Undergraduate & Visiting Researchers

We welcome visiting scholars, visiting students, and undergraduate researchers who are interested in gaining research experience in advanced power electronics and IC design. Please contact us with your CV and a short description of your background and interests.
